Gourmet Tuna Burger with Aji Pepper Sauce, Herbs & Cheese

Ahi Tuna burger patties.

These juicy gourmet Ahi Tuna Burger patties are packed with Asiago cheese, fresh herbs and topped with a roasted Aji Pepper sauce. A dish designed to captivate the most discerning palates!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground ahi tuna steak
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of Asiago cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsely
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp greek yogurt
  • 1 tbp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 potato bread buns
  • 1 cup arugula
  • 1 heirloom or beefsteak tomato
  • 1 small red onion

For the Aji Pepper Sauce:

  • 2 aji peppers
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:

  1. Make the Aji Pepper Sauce: Blend Aji peppers, mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, garlic, lime juice, salt and olive oil until smooth. Refrigerate until ready for use.
  2. Prepare Tuna Patties: In a bowl, mix finely chopped tuna with Asiago cheese, basil, parsley, garlic, Dijon mustard, Greek yogurt, lemon zest, salt and black pepper. Add panko breadcrumbs and mix gently until just combined. Form the mixture into 4 patties and refrigerate for about 20 minutes to firm up.
  3. Cook the Tuna Patties: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the patties for about 2-3 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the Burgers: Toast the buns lightly. Spread aji pepper sauce on the bottom bun. Layer with arugula, tuna patty, slice of tomato and red onion. Add more aji sauce on top, then place the top bun.

Serve with Salt and Vinegar Fries
